Turkiye

"Hello Turkiye!"

  • Turkiye is at the northeast end of the Mediterranean Sea in southeast Europe and southwest Asia. To the north is the Black Sea and to the west is the Aegean Sea. Its neighbors are Greece and Bulgaria to the west, Russia, Ukraine, and Romania to the north and northwest (through the Black Sea), Georgia, Armenia, Azerbaijan, and Iran to the east, and Syria and Iraq to the south. The Dardanelles, the Sea of Marmara, and the Bosporus divide the country. Turkiye in Europe comprises an area about equal to the state of Massachusetts. Turkiye in Asia is about the size of Texas. Its center is a treeless plateau rimmed by mountains.
